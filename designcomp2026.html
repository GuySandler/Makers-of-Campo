We need money for workshops and materials, some will also go towards a charity I haven't decided on yet, feel free to suggest

Goal: Design a product to sell and sell the most to the school (winner by sales, not profit)

Prize: 3D printer (Anycubic Kobra Go with klipper, good for learning) + some fillament (donated by Guy)

Rules:

  • 1 reward per team, this is made to be a solo competiton but you can be in groups.
  • Product must be designed and made by you.
  • Product must be safe, legal, and appropriate for school.
  • Product must be approved by Guy before selling.
  • You can use some of the school's machines to make your product (3d printers, laser engraver, sticker cutter)
  • If you need non-3d printed parts talk to Guy to get it figured out.
  • You are in charge of advertising your product. (we might do bulk advertizeing but don't count on it)
  • 3D printed parts must be posted on printables.com

Ideas:

  • Fidget
  • Desk Organizer
  • Phone stand
  • Keychains
  • Magnets
  • Sticker Sheets
  • Coasters
